structure-generating dynamical theory. Instead, its demonstrated contribution
is as a **stability-preserving constraint framework** for bilinear interaction
systems.

- Exploratory extensions remain non-validated.

## Overall Assessment

Based on discriminative testing, NCFT is a formally coherent framework that

emergent behavior.
